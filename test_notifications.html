🎉 Notification System Test

How to Test the Notification System:
  1. Open the Incresage application in your browser at http://localhost:5176/
  2. Play the game to accumulate Qi and Spirit Stones
  3. When you have enough resources, click the "Attempt Breakthrough" button
  4. You should see a notification appear in the top right corner showing either:
